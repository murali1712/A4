The customer will not hold sender bank responsible, if the remitted funds have neither credited to beneficiary's account, for any reason nor returned by the beneficiary Bank within the time limit prescribed by RBI from time to time.

All NEFT/RTGS transactions are irrevocable. There is no facility for giving STOP payment instruction in respect of remittance through RTGS. The customer shall ensure that there are sufficient funds in his/her/their account to execute the RTGS transaction and the Bank shall not be liable for any consequences arising out of their failure to carry out the instruction due to inadequacy of funds.
